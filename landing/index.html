E2E Encryption

ChaCha20-Poly1305 encryption by default. Not an option — a guarantee. Even we can't read your data.

On-Device AI

A lightweight 1GB AI model runs directly on your phone. Auto-tagging, categorization, link suggestions — all without leaving your device.

100% Offline

Connect notes with [[wikilinks]], build a knowledge graph with auto-backlinks, and auto-extract #hashtags.

Cross-Platform Native

True native performance on iOS, Android, macOS, and Windows. Not Electron — real Flutter native.
